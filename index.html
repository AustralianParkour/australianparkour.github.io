    About

    The APA is made up of representatives elected from Parkour communities around Australia. The APA manages Instructor Qualifications and Insurance, coordinates instructor training programs, and assists communities with governance and representing Parkour

    • Qualifications

      Our Qualifications schema can be found at qualify.parkour.asn.au. These qualifications are administered through the APA Instructor network.

    • Course Development

      APA Senior Instructors can be contracted to develop a training course for your organisation to give them an experience of Parkour, or share how Parkour can be taught safely and effectively.

    • Media

      We share and repost media on our Instagram account @ausparkour, connect with Traceurs around Australia by having a look!
